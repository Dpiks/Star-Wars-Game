STAR WARS - RPG

Welcome to the Star Wars Role Playing Game!!

Game flow and Rules: There are multiple characters to choose. Each character has their own health points(HP), attack power(AP), counter-attack power(CAP). First, you choose your character from the options. The remaining characters become your enemies. You select one at a time from this list and fight that character. The aim of the game is to defeat all your enemies by attacking them. Each time 'Attack' is clicked, your character attacks with AP and enemy counter-attacks with CAP. AP of your character increases by your character's CAP each time. Which means your character becomes powerful every time he attacks. Anytime in the game your character's HP goes to 0 or below, you loose. When you reduce your enemy's HP to 0 or below, that character looses to you and you can select whom you want to attack next. You win by defeating all your enemies.
